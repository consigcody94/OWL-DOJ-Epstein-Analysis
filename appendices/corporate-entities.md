## Delaware Entity Analysis

### Why Delaware?

| Advantage | Relevance |
|-----------|-----------|
| Privacy | No public beneficial owner disclosure |
| Flexibility | Easy formation and dissolution |
| Legal precedent | Well-established corporate law |
| Tax efficiency | No state corporate tax on out-of-state income |

### Red Flags

| Indicator | Present |
|-----------|---------|
| Multiple related entities | Yes |
| Unclear business purpose | Yes |
| Single beneficial owner | Yes |
| High-value assets | Yes |

## Regulatory Implications

### Corporate Transparency Act (2021)

New requirements effective 2024:
- Beneficial ownership reporting
- FinCEN database
- Reduces shell company opacity

**Impact:** Similar structures would be harder to maintain under new requirements.

## Conclusion

The corporate structure served multiple purposes:

**Legitimate:**
- Asset protection
- Tax efficiency
- Operational organization

**Potentially Illegitimate:**
- Obscuring criminal enterprise
- Complicating investigation
- Creating legal barriers

The aviation entities directly facilitated the transportation element of § 2423 charges. The property entities provided venues for abuse. The financial entities remain partially opaque.

**Recommendation:** Full forensic accounting of all entities would provide complete picture of enterprise scope.
