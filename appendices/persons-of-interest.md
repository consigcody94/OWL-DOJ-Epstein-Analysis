## Analysis Framework

### Determining Relevance

| Factor | Weight |
|--------|--------|
| Direct participation in abuse | Critical |
| Facilitation role | High |
| Knowledge and inaction | Moderate |
| Financial benefit | Moderate |
| Mere association | Low |
| Single contact | Minimal |

### Evidentiary Standard

For any individual to be considered culpable:
1. **Evidence of knowledge** of criminal activity
2. **Evidence of participation** or facilitation
3. **Corroboration** from multiple sources
4. **Exclusion** of innocent explanations

The DOJ documents name numerous individuals with varying levels of relevance to the criminal enterprise:

- **2 charged** (Epstein, Maxwell)
- **4+ named staff** (immunity via NPA)
- **Numerous witnesses** (cooperating)

The Maxwell conviction validates the core conspiracy theory. Additional prosecutions of living participants remain theoretically possible for those not covered by NPA immunity provisions.
