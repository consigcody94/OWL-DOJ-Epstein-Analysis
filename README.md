## Legal Framework

### Primary Statute: 18 U.S.C. § 1591

**Sex Trafficking of Children**

Elements Required:
1. ✅ Knowingly recruited, enticed, harbored, transported, provided, obtained, or maintained a person
2. ✅ Knew or recklessly disregarded that the person was under 18
3. ✅ Caused the person to engage in a commercial sex act

**Critical Point**: No force, fraud, or coercion required when victim is a minor.

### Secondary Statute: 18 U.S.C. § 2423

**Transportation of Minors for Illegal Sexual Activity**

Elements Required:
1. ✅ Transported individual in interstate commerce
2. ✅ Individual was under 18
3. ✅ Intent that individual engage in prostitution or criminal sexual activity

**Flight records prove interstate transportation correlated with victim contact.**
